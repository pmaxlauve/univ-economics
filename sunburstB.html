Employment Status based on Education Level (Ethnicity):
The visualization above shows the employment status of the 2019 US labor force based on education level and ethnicity. Each level of the sunburst represents a unique categorical set. Each slice of each layer represents a specific subcategory from the parent category (one level interior). The size of the slice varies based on the percent of the labor force it represents. You can see the percent value as well as a brief description in each slice (you will also see this information as a tooltip when hovering over it).
